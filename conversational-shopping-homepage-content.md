## 3. Problem Section

### Section Eyebrow
**The problem**

### Section Headline
## Online shoppers still struggle to find the right product

### Section Intro
Most ecommerce stores have hundreds or thousands of products, but customers often arrive with vague needs, comparison questions, or uncertainty. Traditional search and filters are not enough when shoppers need guidance.

### Problem Cards

#### Card 1: Product discovery is difficult
Customers rarely know the exact product name, SKU, or category. They describe needs in natural language, but most stores only support keyword search and static filters.

#### Card 2: Product pages leave questions unanswered
Shoppers want to know if a product fits their use case, size, budget, compatibility needs, delivery expectations, or return concerns. If they cannot get answers quickly, they leave.

#### Card 3: Support teams answer repetitive questions
Store owners and support teams repeatedly handle the same questions about products, shipping, returns, stock, sizes, materials, bundles, and compatibility.

#### Card 4: Valuable buying intent is invisible
Search bars and page views do not fully explain what shoppers wanted, why they hesitated, or what objections prevented them from buying.

### Closing Line
Your store already has the product data. The missing layer is a conversational buying experience that turns that data into helpful guidance.
