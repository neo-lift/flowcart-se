✦ Use cases

Built for the moments where shoppers need help

Guided discovery

Help customers find products by need, not keywords

Customers may only know the problem they want to solve. FlowCart turns vague needs into relevant product recommendations.

"I need something for a small balcony garden that doesn't need much maintenance."
Product comparison

Make product decisions easier

When customers are stuck between options, the assistant compares features and recommends the best fit for their priorities.

"What's the difference between these two coffee machines for a small office?"
Fit & compatibility

Reduce uncertainty before checkout

For apparel, parts, accessories, or electronics, customers often need help choosing the right size, fit, or compatible item.

"Will this accessory fit my model?"
Gift recommendations

Turn gift shoppers into buyers

Recommend by recipient, occasion, budget, interest, and delivery deadline — without friction.

"Birthday gift for someone who loves cooking, under €80?"
Pre-sale support

Answer buying questions instantly

Features, materials, delivery, warranty, returns, availability, and alternatives — answered before customers leave.

"How long does delivery take, and can I return it if it doesn't fit?"
Long-tail catalog

Make large catalogs easier to shop

For stores with many SKUs and variations, the assistant helps customers discover products they may otherwise miss.

"Show me products for beginners, in black, under €100."
