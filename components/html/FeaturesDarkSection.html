✦ Features

Everything your store needs to sell through conversation

Ten capabilities built around one promise: help every WooCommerce shopper find the right product faster.

Personalized product recommendations

Recommend products based on needs, preferences, budget, attributes, and stock — all from your WooCommerce catalog.

"Best gift for a beginner gardener under €50?"

Natural-language product search

Shoppers can describe a use case, occasion, material, budget, or compatibility need — and get matching products instantly.

"I need a drill for small home repairs."

Instant product answers

Use descriptions, attributes, variations, stock, prices, and reviews to answer shopper questions in seconds.

"Is this compatible with my model?"

Side-by-side product comparison

Compare price, features, materials, ratings, and suitability — and recommend the best option for each shopper's needs.

Store policy answers

Train your assistant on shipping, returns, payment, warranty, and FAQ pages — and deflect repetitive support questions.

Automatic WooCommerce sync

Names, descriptions, categories, images, prices, inventory, attributes, and variations stay aligned with your live catalog.

Conversation analytics & intent

See what customers ask, which products are recommended, where conversations succeed, and what questions go unanswered.

Customizable chat widget

Customize appearance, welcome message, tone, placement, colors, and CTA behavior so the assistant feels native to your brand.

Lead capture & buying signals

When a shopper shows intent, capture contact info, suggest next actions, or send them to the right product page.

Admin controls

Configure data sources, enable answer types, review unanswered questions, and manage assistant behavior from one dashboard.
