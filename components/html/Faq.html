✦ FAQ

Questions before you get started?

What eCommerce platforms does this work with?
FlowCart works with any eCommerce store. Connect your product catalog and the assistant uses your live data to help shoppers — regardless of which platform you're on.
How long does setup take?
Most stores can connect their catalog and configure the basic assistant quickly. Exact time depends on catalog size, content quality, and customization.
Can the assistant recommend specific products?
Yes. It recommends products based on customer needs, product details, categories, attributes, price, availability, and other catalog data.
Can it answer questions about shipping and returns?
Yes — if you connect the relevant store policy content. FlowCart can use shipping, return, payment, warranty, and FAQ pages to answer common customer questions.
Will it replace my support team?
No. It handles common shopping and product questions, reduces repetitive workload, and helps customers faster. Complex requests can still be routed to your team.
What happens if the assistant doesn't know an answer?
You can configure fallback behavior. The assistant can say it doesn't have enough information, suggest related products, link to a relevant page, or recommend contacting support.
Can I customize the assistant's tone and behavior?
Yes. Define brand instructions, welcome messages, tone, response style, and customer-facing behavior from your dashboard.
Does it support product variations?
Yes. The assistant can use variation data such as sizes, colors, attributes, stock status, and pricing when synced from your store.
Can I see what customers are asking?
Yes. The analytics dashboard helps you review customer questions, product demand signals, unanswered queries, and conversation trends.
Is this only for large stores?
No. Small stores can use it for better product guidance, while larger stores use it to make complex catalogs easier to navigate.
