✦ The problem

Online shoppers still struggle to find the right product

Most stores have thousands of products, but customers arrive with vague needs and comparison questions. Search and filters alone can't guide them — they leave with their cart empty.

Product discovery is difficult

Customers rarely know the exact product name, SKU, or category. They describe needs in natural language — but most stores only support keyword search and static filters.

Product pages leave questions unanswered

Shoppers want to know if a product fits their use case, size, budget, compatibility, delivery, or return needs. If they can't get answers quickly, they leave.

Support answers the same questions, over and over

Store owners and support teams repeatedly handle questions about shipping, returns, stock, sizes, materials, bundles, and compatibility — every single day.

Valuable buying intent stays invisible

Search bars and page views don't reveal what shoppers really wanted, why they hesitated, or which objections prevented them from buying.

Your store already has the product data. The missing layer is a conversational buying experience that turns it into helpful guidance.
