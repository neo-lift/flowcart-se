✦ How it works

Launch your AI shopping assistant in four simple steps

1

Connect WooCommerce

Install the plugin and securely connect your WooCommerce store. Your product catalog and selected pages sync automatically.

2

Train on your products

The assistant learns from titles, descriptions, categories, attributes, variations, prices, stock, and store pages.

3

Customize the experience

Set brand tone, welcome message, widget style, recommended prompts, and fallback behavior.

4

Go live & improve

Add the widget to your storefront, monitor conversations, review analytics, and keep improving answers over time.
