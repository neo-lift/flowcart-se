✦ The problem

Online shoppers still struggle to find the right product

Most stores have thousands of products, but customers arrive with vague needs and comparison questions. Search and filters alone can't guide them — they leave with their cart empty.

Product discovery is difficult

Customers rarely know the exact product name, SKU, or category. They describe needs in natural language — but most stores only support keyword search and static filters.

Product pages leave questions unanswered

Shoppers want to know if a product fits their use case, size, budget, compatibility, delivery, or return needs. If they can't get answers quickly, they leave.

Support answers the same questions, over and over

Store owners and support teams repeatedly handle questions about shipping, returns, stock, sizes, materials, bundles, and compatibility — every single day.

Valuable buying intent stays invisible

Search bars and page views don't reveal what shoppers really wanted, why they hesitated, or which objections prevented them from buying.

Your store already has the product data. The missing layer is a conversational buying experience that turns it into helpful guidance.

✦ FAQ

Questions before you get started?

Does this work with WooCommerce?
Yes. FlowCart is designed for WooCommerce stores and connects to your catalog so the assistant can use product data when helping shoppers.
How long does setup take?
Most stores can connect their WooCommerce catalog and configure the basic assistant quickly. Exact time depends on catalog size, content quality, and customization.
Can the assistant recommend specific products?
Yes. It recommends products based on customer needs, product details, categories, attributes, price, availability, and other catalog data.
Can it answer questions about shipping and returns?
Yes — if you connect the relevant store policy content. FlowCart can use shipping, return, payment, warranty, and FAQ pages to answer common customer questions.
Will it replace my support team?
No. It handles common shopping and product questions, reduces repetitive workload, and helps customers faster. Complex requests can still be routed to your team.
What happens if the assistant doesn't know an answer?
You can configure fallback behavior. The assistant can say it doesn't have enough information, suggest related products, link to a relevant page, or recommend contacting support.
Can I customize the assistant's tone and behavior?
Yes. Define brand instructions, welcome messages, tone, response style, and customer-facing behavior from your dashboard.
Does it support product variations?
Yes. The assistant can use variation data such as sizes, colors, attributes, stock status, and pricing when synced from WooCommerce.
Can I see what customers are asking?
Yes. The analytics dashboard helps you review customer questions, product demand signals, unanswered queries, and conversation trends.
Is this only for large stores?
No. Small stores can use it for better product guidance, while larger stores use it to make complex catalogs easier to navigate.
